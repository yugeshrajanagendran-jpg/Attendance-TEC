Faculty Help Center

How do I mark proctor attendance?

Select the academic date, class hour, and subject. Select individual attendance status for each student (P, A, L, OD, ML) and click "Save Attendance" at the bottom.

Can I edit marked attendance?

Yes. Simply reload the subject, date, and hour. Make adjustments to the statuses and click "Save Attendance" again to overwrite the records.

How do shortage alerts work?

The system calculates student overall participation. Any student who drops below the target (usually 85%) will show up in the Shortage Alerts list. Use the "Notify Parent" button to generate a WhatsApp message.

How do I track assignments?

Click "New Assignment Track", fill in details, and submit. The tracker list will appear on the right column of your dashboard where you can toggle completion for individual students.
