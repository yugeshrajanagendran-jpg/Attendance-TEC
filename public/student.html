Help Center & FAQs

close
Q: How is my attendance computed?

We use standard college rules: (Present + 0.5 * Late) / Total * 100. Absent hours reduce your percentage.

Q: What is the target attendance percentage?

The required attendance to take examinations is 85%. Fallback below 75% will trigger parental notification alerts.

Q: How do I request leave?

Click "Request New Leave" on the dashboard, fill in your details and dates, and submit. Your proctor will review it.

Q: What do OD and ML stand for?

OD stands for On Duty leave (representing official representation of college). ML stands for Medical Leave.
